Key Features

Cluster Management

Manage your container clusters by scaling resources up or down based on demand

Monitoring and Logging

Track the health of your containerized applications using logs and monitor their performance

About Us

This project was inspired by my shared passion for containerization technologies and the desire to simplify the management of Docker clusters. It all started when I encountered the challenges of scaling applications and ensuring their reliability in production environments.

As a developer with diverse backgrounds, I pulled together some resources to create Docker Clusterizer as a solution to these challenges. Through collaboration and innovation, I've developed a platform that empowers developers to efficiently manage their containerized applications.

This project is part of my portfolio for Holberton School, where I am honing my skills in software engineering and pursuing my passion for technology. You can learn more about my journey and the projects I've worked on by visiting Holberton School.
